GIET INSTITUTIONS

Established in 1998, GIET Engineering College is a private college located at Rajahmundry. The college is recognised by AICTE and affiliated with JNT University Kakinada (JNTUK). It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes across various streams, namely, Engineering, Management and Computer Application. Admissions at GIET Engineering College is based on various entrance exams. GIET Engineering College features state-of-the-art facilities and highly qualified and knowledgeable pool of faculty. It has been a pioneer in adopting several pedagogic innovations for the benefit of students. The college also offers placement and internship assistance to students.
